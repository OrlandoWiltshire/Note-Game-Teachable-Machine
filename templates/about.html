About

My Goal: As a musician and music creator, I end up using many different tools to assist me. For example, I use online tuners for guitar and I also use a pitch detctor which inspired me to make this tool that guesses what note you're singing. This tool helps me figure out music theory, melodies, harmonies, etc.

Tools Used: TeachableMachine, Google, W3Schools, PyCharm, ChatGPT
